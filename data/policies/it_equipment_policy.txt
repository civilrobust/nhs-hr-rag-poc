NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
IT EQUIPMENT POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-011
Effective Date: January 2024

1. POLICY STATEMENT
This policy outlines the provision, use, and return of IT equipment issued to ICT Department staff.

2. STANDARD EQUIPMENT ALLOCATION
Desktop Staff: Dell OptiPlex desktop, dual 24-inch monitors, keyboard, mouse, docking station
Mobile/Hybrid Staff: Dell Latitude laptop (i5, 16GB RAM), laptop bag, external mouse, USB-C dock
All Staff: Microsoft Office 365 license, VPN access, MFA token

3. MOBILE PHONES
Issued based on role:
- On-call staff: iPhone or Samsung Galaxy (current model -1)
- Senior managers (Band 7+): Smartphone with data plan
- Monthly data: 10GB standard, 20GB senior roles

4. HOME WORKING EQUIPMENT
Entitled to: Laptop, docking station, external monitor (on request), keyboard, mouse
Home broadband requirement: 30Mbps minimum

5. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
Must: Lock screen when away, never share passwords, report lost/stolen within 2 hours, use VPN for remote access, encrypt sensitive data

6. LEAVING THE TRUST
All equipment returned within final working day. Final salary not processed until equipment returned.

Contact: ict.servicedesk@nhstrust.nhs.uk
