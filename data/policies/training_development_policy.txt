NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
TRAINING & PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-013
Effective Date: January 2024

1. ANNUAL TRAINING BUDGET
Band 3-4: £500 per annum
Band 5: £750 per annum
Band 6: £1,000 per annum
Band 7: £1,500 per annum
Band 8+: £2,000 per annum

2. TYPES SUPPORTED
Mandatory Training: 100% funded (IG, Data Security, Fire Safety)
Technical Certifications: Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, CompTIA (from individual budget)
Professional Development: Leadership, ITIL, PRINCE2, Agile (from individual budget)

3. STUDY LEAVE
Up to 5 days paid study leave per year for approved training
Includes examination days
First exam resit: Paid study leave
Subsequent resits: Annual leave

4. DEGREE-LEVEL SPONSORSHIP
Eligibility: 2+ years service, satisfactory performance
Financial support: Up to 50% tuition (max £4,000/year)
Study leave: Up to 10 days/year paid
Payback clause: Leave within 1 year = repay 100%, within 2 years = 50%

5. PROFESSIONAL MEMBERSHIPS
BCS, CITP, CISSP memberships funded if relevant to role

Contact: ict.training@nhstrust.nhs.uk
