NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
ON-CALL POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-012
Effective Date: January 2024

1. POLICY STATEMENT
Establishes fair on-call arrangements for ICT staff providing out-of-hours support for critical NHS services.

2. ON-CALL ALLOWANCE
Primary on-call: £250 per week
Secondary on-call: £150 per week
Bank Holiday weeks: Additional 50% premium
Christmas/New Year weeks: Double rate

3. CALL-OUT PAYMENTS
Remote call-out: Minimum 1 hour at time-and-a-half
On-site attendance: Minimum 4 hours at double-time
Travel time paid at standard rate, mileage 45p/mile

4. RESPONSE TIME REQUIREMENTS
P1 Critical: Acknowledge 15 mins, action 30 mins
P2 Major: Acknowledge 30 mins, action 1 hour
P3 Minor: Next working day

5. AVAILABILITY REQUIREMENTS
Must be: Contactable within 5 mins, able to access VPN within 15 mins, within 30 mins of home, fit to work

6. HEALTH AND WELLBEING
If called out overnight 2+ hours: Late start permitted next day
If called out 4+ hours: Day off permitted

Contact: ict.oncall@nhstrust.nhs.uk
