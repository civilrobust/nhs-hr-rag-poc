NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - FLEXIBLE WORKING POLICY  
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-008
Effective Date: January 2024

ELIGIBILITY
Staff with 26 weeks continuous service may request flexible working arrangements including:
- Part-time hours
- Compressed hours (full-time hours in fewer days)
- Flexitime
- Job sharing
- Term-time working

APPLICATION PROCESS
Submit written request to line manager including:
- Proposed work pattern
- Start date
- Impact on role and team
- How work will be covered

REQUEST CONSIDERATION
Manager will arrange meeting within 28 days to discuss request. Decision provided in writing within 14 days considering:
- Service delivery requirements
- Impact on team
- Customer needs
- Cost implications

FLEXITIME SYSTEM
Core hours: 10:00-15:00 (must be present)
Flexible hours: 07:00-10:00 and 15:00-19:00
Staff accrue time and may take up to 1 day flexi-leave per month.

ICT-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS
Flexible arrangements must ensure:
- On-call rota coverage maintained
- Service desk staffing levels met
- Critical systems support available
- Team meetings attended

TRIAL PERIOD
New flexible arrangements have a 3-month trial period. Either party may revert to previous pattern with 4 weeks notice.
