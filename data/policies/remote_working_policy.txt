NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - REMOTE WORKING POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-003
Effective Date: January 2024

ELIGIBILITY
ICT staff may work remotely up to 3 days per week subject to:
- Successful completion of probation period
- Role suitability for remote work
- Manager approval
- Adequate home broadband (minimum 30Mbps)

REMOTE WORKING DAYS
Staff must maintain at least 2 days per week office presence for team collaboration, meetings, and physical infrastructure tasks.

EQUIPMENT AND SECURITY
Trust will provide:
- Laptop with encrypted hard drive
- VPN access to Trust systems
- Mobile phone for work communications

Staff must:
- Use only Trust-provided equipment for work
- Never share login credentials
- Ensure secure home working environment
- Lock screens when away from desk

ON-CALL REQUIREMENTS
Staff on-call must work from location with reliable internet. If remote working impacts on-call response times, office presence may be required.

DATA PROTECTION
All NHS patient data and confidential information must be handled according to Trust Information Governance policies. No patient data may be stored on local devices.

HEALTH AND SAFETY
Staff must complete a home workstation assessment and ensure ergonomic setup meets HSE guidelines.
