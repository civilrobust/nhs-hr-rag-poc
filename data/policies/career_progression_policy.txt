NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
CAREER PROGRESSION POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-020
Effective Date: January 2024

1. NHS BAND STRUCTURE
Band 3: IT Trainee, Service Desk Analyst
Band 4: Service Desk Senior Analyst, Junior Technician
Band 5: ICT Technician, Systems Administrator, Developer
Band 6: Senior Technician, Team Leader, Senior Developer
Band 7: ICT Manager, Solutions Architect, Principal Engineer
Band 8a: Service Manager, Senior Manager
Band 8b: Head of Service
Band 8c/d: Director of Digital

2. PROMOTION CRITERIA
Time in role: Minimum 12 months (exceptions for exceptional performance)
Performance: Satisfactory or above in last 2 appraisals
Skills: Demonstrating skills at next band level
Vacancy: Advertised post available at higher band

3. ACTING UP OPPORTUNITIES
Cover for vacant post: Minimum 4 weeks duration
Acting up allowance: Difference between bands (pro-rata if partial duties)
Development opportunity: Builds experience for promotion
No automatic promotion: Must apply through normal process

4. DEVELOPMENT PATHWAYS
Technical route: Specialist/expert (Bands 5-7)
Management route: Team leader to manager (Bands 6-8)
Hybrid route: Technical leadership (Bands 6-8)
Project route: Project manager to programme manager (Bands 6-8)

5. SECONDMENTS
Internal secondments: 6-12 months typical
External secondments: NHS Digital, other Trusts
Development opportunity: Broaden experience
Return to substantive post: Guaranteed at end of secondment

6. CAREER CONVERSATIONS
Annual appraisal: Discuss career aspirations
Personal development plan: Identify development needs
Mentor support: Available for career guidance
Talent management: High performers identified for development
