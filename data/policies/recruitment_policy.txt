NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
RECRUITMENT POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-018
Effective Date: January 2024

1. VACANCY APPROVAL
All vacancies require Director of Digital approval
Business case: Justify need, budget confirmed
Job description: Up-to-date, banding verified

2. ADVERTISING
NHS Jobs: Minimum 2 weeks posting
External sites: LinkedIn, Indeed (for specialist roles)
Internal opportunities: Advertised to existing staff first (5 days)

3. SELECTION PROCESS
Shortlisting: Minimum 2 panel members, criteria-based
Interviews: Structured questions, competency-based
Assessment: Technical test for technical roles (1 hour maximum)
Panel: Minimum 2 interviewers, diversity where possible

4. PRE-EMPLOYMENT CHECKS
DBS: Standard DBS for all ICT staff (6-week processing)
References: 2 references covering last 3 years employment
Right to work: Original documents verified
Occupational health: Clearance required before start date
Qualifications: Certificates verified for essential qualifications

5. OFFERS
Conditional offer: Subject to satisfactory pre-employment checks
Start date: Minimum 4 weeks notice period (negotiate if needed)
Contract: Issued within 1 week of unconditional offer

6. ONBOARDING
IT account creation: 5 days before start date
Equipment ordered: 2 weeks before start date
Induction schedule: First week planned
Buddy assigned: Experienced team member

Contact: ict.recruitment@nhstrust.nhs.uk
