NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
TRAVEL & EXPENSES POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-016
Effective Date: January 2024

1. MILEAGE RATES
Personal vehicle: 45p per mile (first 10,000 miles per year), 25p thereafter
Motorcycles: 24p per mile
Bicycles: 20p per mile

2. PUBLIC TRANSPORT
Rail: Standard class, advance purchase when possible
Air: Economy class only (UK domestic flights require Director approval)
Taxi: Only when public transport unavailable or journey after 21:00

3. ACCOMMODATION
Maximum rates:
London: £150 per night
Other UK cities: £100 per night
Budget hotels preferred (Premier Inn, Travelodge)
Booking via Trust travel provider mandatory

4. SUBSISTENCE
Breakfast: £8 (if not included in hotel)
Lunch: £10
Evening meal: £20
Maximum daily total: £38

5. CLAIMS PROCESS
Submit via ESR within 2 months of expense incurred
Receipts required for all claims over £5
Mileage: Postcode-to-postcode calculation
Payment: Processed with next monthly salary

Contact: ict.finance@nhstrust.nhs.uk
