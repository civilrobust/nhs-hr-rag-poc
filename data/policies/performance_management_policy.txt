NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-010
Effective Date: January 2024

PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
All ICT staff are expected to:
- Meet service level agreements
- Maintain technical competency
- Respond to incidents within target times
- Complete mandatory training
- Demonstrate Trust values

ANNUAL APPRAISAL
All staff receive annual appraisal including:
- Review of previous year's objectives
- Assessment of competencies
- Identification of development needs
- Agreement of objectives for coming year
- Career development discussion

OBJECTIVE SETTING
Objectives should be SMART:
- Specific: Clear and well-defined
- Measurable: Quantifiable outcomes
- Achievable: Realistic given resources
- Relevant: Aligned to service goals
- Time-bound: Clear deadline

PERFORMANCE CONCERNS
If performance falls below expected standards:
1. Informal discussion with line manager
2. Performance improvement plan (3 months)
3. Formal capability procedure if no improvement

PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN
Includes:
- Specific areas requiring improvement
- Support and training to be provided
- Regular review meetings (fortnightly)
- Clear timescales and success criteria

EXCEPTIONAL PERFORMANCE
High performers may be recognized through:
- Annual staff awards
- Accelerated development opportunities
- Acting up opportunities
- Performance-related bonus (subject to Trust budget)

PROBATIONARY REVIEWS
New staff have performance reviews at 3 and 6 months before confirmation of employment.
