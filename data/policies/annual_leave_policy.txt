NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-002
Effective Date: January 2024

ANNUAL LEAVE ENTITLEMENT
Standard entitlement (in addition to 8 public holidays):
- Upon commencement: 27 days (pro-rata for part-time staff)
- After 5 years NHS service: 29 days
- After 10 years NHS service: 33 days

The leave year runs from 1st April to 31st March.

BOOKING PROCEDURES
Advance notice requirements:
- Single days: Minimum 2 weeks notice
- 2-5 days: Minimum 4 weeks notice
- 6+ days: Minimum 8 weeks notice
- Christmas/New Year: Book by 30th September

SERVICE COVERAGE
To ensure service continuity:
- At least 70% of each ICT team must be present
- No more than 2 staff from same specialist area on leave simultaneously
- Critical periods may have additional restrictions

CARRYING FORWARD LEAVE
Up to 5 days annual leave may be carried forward to next leave year with line manager approval. Unused leave not carried forward will be forfeited at year end.
