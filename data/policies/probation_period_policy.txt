NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
PROBATION PERIOD POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-014
Effective Date: January 2024

1. PROBATION DURATION
Standard: 6 months for Bands 3-6
Internal transfers: 3 months
Extended (if concerns): Up to 3 additional months (9 months max)

2. REVIEW MEETINGS
Week 2: Informal check-in
Month 1: Formal review
Month 3: Formal review
Month 6: Final review (Pass, Extend, or Fail decision)

3. ASSESSMENT CRITERIA
Technical skills: Competence, problem-solving, learning ability
Professional behavior: Timekeeping, communication, response to feedback
Values: Patient-focused, teamwork, integrity, initiative

4. PASSING PROBATION
Permanent contract confirmed in writing
Full sick pay entitlement commences
Enhanced maternity/paternity pay eligible
Access to salary sacrifice schemes

5. NOTICE PERIODS DURING PROBATION
Trust to employee: 1 week
Employee to Trust: 1 week (months 1-3), 2 weeks (months 4-6)
