NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT
IT SECURITY & ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-017
Effective Date: January 2024

1. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
Minimum 12 characters
Combination of upper, lower, numbers, special characters
Changed every 90 days
Never shared or written down
Different for each system

2. DATA PROTECTION
Patient data: Only access if required for role
Encryption: Mandatory for all removable media
Email: Do not send patient data without encryption
Printing: Collect immediately, do not leave sensitive documents

3. ACCEPTABLE USE
Personal use: Reasonable during breaks only
Prohibited: Illegal downloads, streaming, gambling, adult content
Social media: No Trust business on personal accounts without approval
Email: Professional tone, Trust disclaimer applies

4. SECURITY INCIDENTS
Report immediately: Lost devices, suspected breach, phishing emails
Contact: IT Security team via extension 2222
Timeline: Within 2 hours of discovery
Consequences: Disciplinary action for unreported incidents

5. REMOTE WORKING SECURITY
VPN mandatory for all external access
Public WiFi: Avoid for sensitive work, use VPN if essential
Home network: WPA2 encryption minimum, strong password
Device security: Lock screen when unattended, store securely

Contact: itsecurity@nhstrust.nhs.uk
