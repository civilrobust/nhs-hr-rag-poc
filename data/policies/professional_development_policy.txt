NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-007
Effective Date: January 2024

COMMITMENT TO DEVELOPMENT
The Trust supports ongoing professional development for ICT staff to maintain skills and deliver high-quality services.

TRAINING ENTITLEMENT
All ICT staff are entitled to:
- Minimum 5 days training per year
- Annual appraisal to identify development needs
- Access to e-learning platforms
- Support for professional certifications

APPROVED CERTIFICATIONS
Trust will fund relevant certifications including:
- Microsoft (Azure, M365, Windows Server)
- Cisco (CCNA, CCNP)
- CompTIA (Security+, Network+)
- ITIL Foundation and Practitioner
- VMware
- AWS/Cloud certifications

Funding covers exam fees and approved training courses. Staff must achieve certification within 12 months.

STUDY LEAVE
Staff may request:
- Up to 5 days paid study leave per year for exam preparation
- Time off to attend courses (subject to service needs)
- Flexible working around training schedules

PROFESSIONAL BODY MEMBERSHIP
Trust will pay annual membership fees for one relevant professional body per employee (e.g., BCS, CompTIA).

TRAINING COMMITMENT
Staff receiving certification funding must:
- Remain employed for 12 months after qualification
- Share knowledge with team
- Apply skills in current role

If staff leave within 12 months, pro-rata training costs may be recovered.
