NHS TRUST ICT DEPARTMENT - MATERNITY & PATERNITY LEAVE POLICY
Policy Reference: HR-ICT-009
Effective Date: January 2024

MATERNITY LEAVE ENTITLEMENT
All pregnant staff are entitled to 52 weeks maternity leave:
- First 26 weeks: Ordinary Maternity Leave
- Last 26 weeks: Additional Maternity Leave

MATERNITY PAY
With 12 months continuous service:
- First 8 weeks: Full pay
- Next 18 weeks: Half pay plus Statutory Maternity Pay
- Final 13 weeks: Statutory Maternity Pay only

NOTIFICATION
Staff must notify manager by 15 weeks before due date providing:
- Expected week of childbirth
- Intended leave start date
- MATB1 certificate from midwife

PATERNITY LEAVE
Partners are entitled to:
- 2 weeks paid paternity leave
- To be taken within 56 days of birth
- Paid at statutory paternity pay rate

SHARED PARENTAL LEAVE
Parents may share up to 50 weeks leave and 37 weeks pay between them if both meet eligibility criteria.

KEEPING IN TOUCH
Staff may work up to 10 'Keeping in Touch' days during maternity leave without losing maternity pay. This helps maintain skills and aid return to work.

RETURN TO WORK
Staff returning from maternity leave are entitled to:
- Return to same role if leave under 26 weeks
- Return to same or similar role if leave over 26 weeks
- Phased return arrangements if requested
- Breastfeeding breaks and facilities
